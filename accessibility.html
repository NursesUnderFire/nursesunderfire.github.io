Accessibility Statement

We want everyone to be able to learn about and take action on workplace violence prevention. If you encounter a barrier, please tell us so we can fix it quickly.

Our commitments

  • Semantic HTML with clear headings, labels, and ARIA attributes where appropriate.
  • Color contrast that meets WCAG AA guidelines.
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation, skip links, and focus styles.
  • Descriptive link text and alternative text for informative images.

How to report an issue

Email nursesunderfire1@gmail.com with the page URL, a brief description of the problem, and your assistive technology (if applicable). We aim to acknowledge reports within 3 business days.

Ongoing improvements

  • Regular audits of new content for keyboard and screen reader support.
  • Testing forms with and without JavaScript enabled to ensure fallback paths.
  • Providing transcripts or summaries for future audio or video content.
