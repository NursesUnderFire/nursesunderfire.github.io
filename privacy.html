Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share with Nurses Under Fire, whether you visit our site, join our newsletter, or submit your story.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide in forms (name, email) for newsletter updates or advocacy coordination.
  • Engagement data such as page views and anonymized analytics that help us understand which resources are most helpful.
  • Testimonials and stories that you voluntarily submit to highlight the realities of workplace violence.

How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver newsletters, campaign updates, and legislative alerts you opted into.
  • Improve content relevance and accessibility using aggregated, anonymized analytics.
  • Respond to inquiries and coordinate advocacy actions with lawmakers and partners.

Testimonials and Story Submissions

We never publish testimonials or lived-experience stories without your permission. When stories are shared publicly:

  • We default to anonymizing names, locations, and identifiable details unless you explicitly request attribution.
  • You may withdraw or update a testimonial at any time by emailing nursesunderfire1@gmail.com.
  • We may lightly edit submissions for clarity while preserving meaning and context.

Cookies and Analytics

Our site may use minimal cookies for performance and accessibility preferences. Any analytics tools we use aggregate data and do not identify individual visitors.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited sharing occurs only with trusted service providers (such as email platforms) to deliver the services you request, and they must protect your information under confidentiality obligations.

Your Choices

  • You can unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in the footer or by contacting us.
  • Request updates, corrections, or deletion of your personal information by emailing nursesunderfire1@gmail.com.
  • Adjust browser settings to manage cookies if you prefer.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests, email nursesunderfire1@gmail.com. We aim to respond within five business days.
